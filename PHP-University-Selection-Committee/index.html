  • IITU was established on April 2, 2009 on behalf of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan - Nursultan Abishevich Nazarbayev.
  • On May 29, 2009, the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan issued a state license for educational activities to the University.
  • Cisco Laboratory - in Almaty in summer 2009, the university signed a memorandum of understanding and cooperation in the field of information and communication technologies with Cisco. This was done as part of the active development of cooperation with the largest IT companies in order to provide the students of the International University of Information Technologies with the best opportunities for obtaining high-quality specialized education.
  • Cloud Computing of Innogrid - On August 9, 2011, a laboratory of open systems and cloud computing was established in close cooperation with the Chinese company Huawei, the Zerde infocommunication holding and the International University of Information Technology.
  • The Apple Training Center is an official Apple certified laboratory that develops mobile applications for the IOS operating system. The laboratory was opened in 2011 at the International University of Information Technology. The laboratory belongs to the Department of Information Systems.
  • Also in 2011, there were such significant events in the history of the university as a Memorandum with JSC NIT on July 16, 2011 on mutual understanding with Universiti Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and a Memorandum with Graduate IT Systems Management School, Riga on September 15, 2011 on mutual understanding with Riga Technical University Riga. Latvia
  • On May 8, 2013, the University received scientific and scientific and technical accreditation from the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan and on July 4, 2018 passed the re-accreditation procedure.
  • June 25, 2013 A ceremonial presentation of diplomas to the first graduates of the International University of Information Technologies took place in Almaty. Deputy Akim of Almaty Zauresh Amanzholova, Vice President of iCarnegie Michael Bridzhez, Ambassador of Malaysia to Kazakhstan Dato Ahmad Razidi Bin Hazizi took part in the award ceremony.
  • The 3D graphics and animation laboratory was opened at the International University of Information Technologies in 2013. Despite this, the laboratory managed to create several high-quality products for the Kazakhstan market. The main focus of the laboratory is 3D Mapping Show, as well as the creation of video presentations and various 3D projects.
  • In 2015, IITU students took first place in 1/4 of the world championship in sports programming ACM ICPC 2015. This victory allowed the team to reach the semifinals of the world championship in sports programming ACM ICPC 2015-2016, which was held on December 6 in Tashkent.
  • Also, IITU students took first place in the final of the international technology competition Imagine Cup in the Game development category. The competition was held in three nominations: socially significant projects (World Citizenship), innovative applications (Innovation) and games (Games).
  • On December 11, 2015, a collective amateur radio station with the call sign UN9GWA was opened at IITU. The MUIT collective radio station is equipped with modern radio equipment from ICOM and BARRETT firms, and efficient antennas. The IUIT radio station is the first and only collective radio station among the universities of Kazakhstan at the moment.
